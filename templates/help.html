Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?

You can cancel anytime by going to “My Bookings” and clicking on the cancel option. Refund policies may vary depending on service providers.

I didn’t get my ticket confirmation. What do I do?

Please check your email and spam folder. If you still haven’t received it, contact us through the Contact page.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, internet banking, and popular wallets.

Is customer support available 24/7?

Yes, our support team is available all day, every day to assist you.
